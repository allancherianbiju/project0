Useless facts about Allan

Among other things...

With the four kingdoms in sight, Allan the Noble is on a quest to conquer them all and be back in time for chow.

S.P.E.C.I.A.L. Details
Strength Astounding. Known throughout the lands for the impressive performance put up by the man in the septennial boulder rolling tournament.
Perception Sharp. Tales have been told of Allan's mastery over the physical plane and his ability to sense the slightest variations in his surroundings, which has enabled him time and again to thwart off attacks from the assassins sent by the Golden King.
Endurance Sempiternal. Don't let his lean appearance fool you. The man has survived the Himalayan blizzards and the scorching heat of the Sahara desert, while surviving on rations. He has swam across the English channel on multiple occassions in order to raise money for charity.
Charisma Living embodiment of all that is debonair in this cold and dark world, Allan is knowing for his sharp wit and his ability to turn any conversation into one about pineapples and soil ranked on the basis of percolation.
Intelligence It's over 9000! His ability to account for each and every move made by his enemies is only matched by his never ending thirst for knowledge.
Agility His lean frame has granted him with abilities that have since been deemed, superhuman. He is the only human to have completed the trans-universe triathalon in under 12 parasecs.
Luck Extremely! While many choose to believe that he is the byproduct of the cosmos trying to bring balance to the universe, Allan chooses to not consider that possibility but rather to forge ahead and make his own luck.
